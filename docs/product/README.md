## ðŸš€ Product Overview

TimeClout is an AI-powered team scheduling and workforce management platform that helps organizations:

- **Schedule shifts intelligently** with AI-powered auto-fill
- **Manage leave requests** with automated approval workflows
- **Track schedule distribution** with statistics and insights
- **Ensure fairness** with balanced shift distribution
- **Scale efficiently** from small teams to large enterprises

**Target Users**: Service industries, retail, healthcare, hospitality, manufacturing, and any organization with complex scheduling needs.
